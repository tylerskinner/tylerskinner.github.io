About Me

I am a fourth-year doctoral candidate in Sport Management and Policy at the University of Georgia. Prior to joining the Ph.D. program, I received graduate degrees at the University of Pennsylvania and Wake Forest University and an undergraduate degree at the University of Alabama.

My research interests broadly revolve around topics related to strategic management and managerial economics using sport data. I am currently involved in empirical projects examining managerial labor markets, strategic leadership, and executive compensation. I have been featured in media outlets such as Fox Business Network and Georgia Public Broadcasting (National Public Radio). I have also presented my research at academic conferences such as the North American Society for Sport Management, Missouri Valley Economic Association, Applied Sport Management Association, and College Sports Research Institute.

My expected graduation is Spring 2024 and I plan to be on the 2023-2024 academic job market. Please click here for my CV, and feel free to contact me.
