About Me

I am a third-year doctoral candidate in Sport Management and Policy at the University of Georgia. Prior to joining the Ph.D. program, I received graduate degrees at the University of Pennsylvania and Wake Forest University and an undergraduate degree at the University of Alabama. My research interests include managerial impact on performance, strategic leadership, executive compensation, and sports economics. I have been featured in media outlets such as Fox Business Network and Georgia Public Broadcasting (National Public Radio). I have also presented my research at academic conferences such as the North American Society for Sport Management, Missouri Valley Economic Association, Applied Sport Management Association, and College Sports Research Institute.
